But her mind is tormented, she is unable to cope with the fears and nervous tension caused by strange memories, dreams and visions. Maybe she should go to Wonderland? It always helped ... Alice goes there in search of what she can not find in the "real" world - safety, knowledge and the truth about her past. But during her absence, Wonderland has changed a lot. Something terrible has happened, and a terrible evil has now taken up residence in the once beautiful refuge. Will Alice be able to save Wonderland, and herself, from all-consuming madness?

Eleven years ago, Alice's parents died in a terrible fire, which left a profound mark on the child's mental state. The girl ended up in Ruthledge Psychiatric Hospital, where she struggled with the demons that overcame her, slipping away into a world of her own fantasies - Wonderland. After ten years of treatment, Alice recovered enough to be discharged from the hospital, although she has not completely gotten rid of the effects of the psychological trauma received as a result of the tragic event.
